Brawl Stars

General Info

Brawl Stars is a 3v3 (most of the time) multiplayer, shoot 'em up, party brawl game. In this mobile game you one of the many brawlers each with their own attack, "super" and "star powers". you charge up your super by attacking others and you unlock brawlers and star powers from "brawl boxes". Star powers give extra abilities when playing. Brawl boxes are this games version of loot chests. After choosing a brawler and possibly a star power you choose a mode to brawl in. There are many modes including bounty, heist, and showdown. In most of the modes you fight in a small 3v3 arena, but in some modes you fight with 9 other brawlers in a big arena.

Brawlers

Brawlers are the the people you fight with. There are 30 brawlers in the game. Each brawler has their own attack and super. They have two star powers each. There are 2 types of supers "one and done" and spawnables. Spawnable supers have there own stats. Each brawler has their own voice lines.
